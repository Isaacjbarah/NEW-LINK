The Causes Of Climate Change

There are many different ways that climate change is caused. So here are some of the main causes of climate change.

Burning Fossil Fuels

Fossil fuels, especially in America, are the biggest reason why climate change is so bad. America alone contributes 14% of gas emissions in the entire world, which puts us in the top 3 countries that emit gas into the atmosphere. The debate over the solutions to climate change has been debated for years, and now we are seeing the effects of it. 2023 breaks a record for being the warmest year on record. If last summer was super hot, wait until you see it this summer.

Deforestaion/Forest Fires

Deforestation/Forest Fires is another big issue that needs to be addressed. Trees are a storage for carbon dioxide, which is what we need to survive, but when thousands of trees are being cut down. It releases that carbon dioxide into the atmosphere. We saw what happened when Canada had a huge forest fire that traveled to America. The effects of the forest fires were so bad that schools were forced to close.

Industry

Many industries are again another major problem that needs fixing. Industries around the country emit a long range of greenhouse gasses into the air. One example is when oil companies go bankrupt, they leave hundreds of oil wells wide open. Releasing large amounts of methane into the air, which is much worse than other gasses.
